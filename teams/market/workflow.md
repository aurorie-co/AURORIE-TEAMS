# Market Workflow

## Campaign Launch
Trigger: new campaign or promotional push

Steps:
1. market-lead reviews campaign goals and assigns work
2. market-content drafts copy and creative briefs
3. market-seo reviews for search optimization
4. Write output to `.claude/workspace/artifacts/market/<task-id>/`

## Performance Report
Trigger: scheduled reporting cycle or ad-hoc analytics request

Steps:
1. market-lead scopes the reporting request
2. market-analytics gathers data and produces report
3. Write output to `.claude/workspace/artifacts/market/<task-id>/`
