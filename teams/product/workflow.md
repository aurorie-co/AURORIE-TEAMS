# Product Workflow

## PRD Creation
Trigger: new feature or initiative requiring a product requirements document

Steps:
1. product-lead scopes the initiative and assigns work
2. product-ux conducts UX research and writes design specifications
3. product-pm writes PRD incorporating UX findings
4. Write output to `.claude/workspace/artifacts/product/<task-id>/`

## Roadmap Planning
Trigger: quarterly planning cycle or strategic pivot

Steps:
1. product-lead gathers business goals and constraints
2. product-pm synthesizes priorities into roadmap document
3. Write output to `.claude/workspace/artifacts/product/<task-id>/`
